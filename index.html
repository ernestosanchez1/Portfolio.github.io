Optimizing Airport Efficiency Using Machine Learning

Predicting Taxi Demand Analysis at Vancouver International Airport (YVR)

The current taxi service system at YVR faces several challenges. The existing taxi stand relies on a staff member who manually gauges the length of the taxi queue, making it difficult to accurately predict the demand for taxis. This project aims to leverage predictive analytics to forecast the taxi pick-up demand on an hourly basis. By utilizing machine learning, we can accurately anticipate the fluctuating demand for taxis at different times of the day.
